## Thesis

Creativity in ACM does **not** require a separate executive “creativity organ.” It emerges when memory can:

1. **Recombine** existing fragments into novel temporary structures (M13)  
2. **Map analogies** across domains despite surface difference (M14)  
3. Optionally **simulate** prospective paths (M12) and **predict** likelihood (M11)

## Why not a standalone Creativity organ (now)

A standalone creativity module tends to become unconstrained generation or planning. ACM keeps creativity as a **memory property**: novel but lineage-bound combinations and structural transfers that remain observable and never rewrite history.

## Future Creativity milestone

A later Creativity organ (if any) would **orchestrate** recombination + analogy + simulation under attention/priority — still without decision making. Not authorized in M13/M14.

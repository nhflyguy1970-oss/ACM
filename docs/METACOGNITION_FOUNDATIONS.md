## What metacognition is

Metacognition is cognition about cognition: observing and evaluating one’s own mental products without pretending that observation *is* learning or planning.

## Why Reflection is the first metacognitive organ

Memory structures (Identity → Experience → Concept → Association → Remembering) make reconstruction possible. Without Reflection, ACM cannot *judge* those reconstructions. Reflection is the first organ that:

- Self-observes reconstructions  
- Self-evaluates confidence / conflict / sufficiency  
- Records that evaluation as new history  

## Intellectual honesty

Valid metacognitive conclusions include uncertainty and ignorance. Forced certainty is a failure mode.

## Confidence

Confidence assessment here is **evaluative**, not silent overwrite of Concept strength. Observed assessments become artifacts / harness metrics. Future Learning may apply adaptations.

## Why remembering reconstructs

Human recall rebuilds a present-tense cognitive scene from partial activations. ACM mirrors that: reconstituting meaning under Identity, Goals, Context, and Working Memory — not emitting a stored row.

## Why retrieval is insufficient

Lookup optimizes for permanence and exactness. Cognition optimizes for *useful presence*. Blind retrieval:

- ignores identity-relevant emphasis  
- ignores goal bias  
- cannot represent legitimate competing memories  
- tempts history rewrite when answers “update”

## Effects

| Influence | Reconstruction role |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Identity | Privileges self-schemata; owns Who-am-I paths |
| Goals | Biases unfinished threads |
| Context | Colors which attributes / experiences participate |
| Attention | Scales seed energy |
| Associations | Shape which related meanings join |
| Salience / temporal | Prefer recently or strongly lived evidence |

## Confidence & ambiguity

Confidence is an observable cognitive quantity. Ambiguity is first-class when competitors are close. Certainty is never forced for social convenience.

## Historical integrity

Reconstruction may change **accessibility** (Concept strength, Association reinforce-on-recall, overlays). It must not mutate Experience content. History remains the Experience organ’s ownership.

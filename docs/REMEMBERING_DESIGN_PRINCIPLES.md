## Remembering is reconstruction — not retrieval

| Is | Is not |
|----|--------|
| Cognitive reconstruction from current state | Database lookup |
| Cue-driven activation + spread | Keyword search |
| Influence by Identity / Goals / Context / WM | Vector RAG as the design center |
| Preservation of historical integrity | History rewriting |

Every recollection is shaped by **who ACM is**, **what it is trying to do**, **where attention sits**, **which Concepts and Associations fire**, and **how confident those structures are**. The same cue under different contexts may emphasize differently while Experience content remains immutable.

## Historical integrity

- Experiences are never rewritten by Remembering.  
- Accessibility may cool later (future Forgetting) without deletion.  
- Corrections birth new Experiences (M2); Remembering may surface competing actives with uncertainty.

## Influence sources (mandatory)

Identity · Goals · Attention · Context · Concept activation · Association activation · Salience · Temporal proximity · Confidence · Working Memory · Current cognitive state.

## User trust

Public explanations remain **template classes** (no chain-of-thought, no prompts). Observability shows activation paths and confidence — not fabricated reasoning dumps.

## Future organs

| Organ | Relationship |
|-------|----------------|
| Reflection | Will evaluate reconstructions; must not be absorbed into Remembering |
| Learning | Will improve cueability / strengths continuously |
| Activation Architecture | Shared; Remembering is the first consumer |

**Learning ≠ self-improvement.** Continuous adaptation of strengths is allowed; changing activation *policy / architecture* requires future explicit user authorization.

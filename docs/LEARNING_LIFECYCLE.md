# Learning Lifecycle — ACM

**Status:** Canonical for L0 (design only)  
**Companions:** [`LEARNING_ARCHITECTURE.md`](LEARNING_ARCHITECTURE.md) · [`CONCEPT_LIFECYCLE.md`](CONCEPT_LIFECYCLE.md) · [`ASSOCIATION_MODEL.md`](ASSOCIATION_MODEL.md)

Learning adaptations themselves have a lifecycle — distinct from Experience immutability and from Concept stages.

---

## Stages of an Adaptation

```
candidate (from Reflection / repetition / sleep proposal)
  → proposed (high-impact) OR applying (low-impact)
  → active (applied)
  → reinforced | weakened
  → generalized | specialized
  → consolidated (sleep-confirmed)
  → dormant
  → retired | rolled_back
```

---

## Birth

Triggered when Reflective outcomes or repeated encode evidence cross learnability thresholds. Creates Adaptation Record citing lineage.

## Reinforcement

Congruent successes / Reflective `consistency`/`sufficient` increase magnitude within caps (automatic) or reinforce an existing adaptation.

## Weakening

Conflicts, Reflective `contradiction`, failed predictions (future), or disuse reduce adaptation strength — not Experience deletion.

## Generalization

Shared features across exemplars → broaden Concept prototype weights / Association neighborhoods. Prefer **proposals** when crossing domain boundaries (future Analogy).

## Specialization

Discriminating evidence splits overly broad lessons (link to Concept split *proposals*).

## Consolidation

Sleep reviews Adaptation candidates: apply low-impact; queue high-impact for assent. Consolidation prefers pattern-supported changes over one-offs (spacing / offline practice — well-supported behaviorally).

## Transfer

Applying a lesson in a new neighborhood. Requires Association distance + future analogical structure. L0 records the need; does not specify algorithm beyond Activation reuse.

## Dormancy

Unused adaptations cool; they remain reverseable and queryable under `what_have_i_learned(include_dormant=…)`.

## Retirement

Superseded by clearer adaptations; lineage preserved. Never silent.

---

## Interaction with Reflection

| Reflection outcome | Typical Learning response |
|--------------------|---------------------------|
| `sufficient` + `consistency` | Reinforce related Concept/Association (auto if small) |
| `contradiction` | Weaken or propose contest; maybe question → gather experience |
| `insufficient_evidence` / `uncertainty` | Often **no** adaptation; record abstention |
| `question` / `hypothesis` | May open goal bias or mark provisional Concept — not assertive identity change |
| `pattern` | Candidate generalization |

Reflection remains the evaluator. Learning remains the adapter.

---

## Interaction with future Forgetting

Forgetting cools **accessibility**. Learning may still hold dormant adaptations. History Experiences never erased by either organ.

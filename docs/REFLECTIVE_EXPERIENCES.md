## Why reflections become immutable Experiences

Evaluation that is not recorded cannot be trusted, audited, or learned from. ACM records Reflection as new Experiences with `CognitiveKind.REFLECTION` and `reflects_on` lineage to primary evidence (or the Reconstruction’s primary participating Experience).

## Lineage

```
Remembering reconstruction (ephemeral cognitive product)
        ↓ evaluates
Reflective Experience (immutable) — reflects_on → prior Experience
```

Corrections still use `revises` (M2). Reflection never uses `revises` to overwrite content; it **annotates** via lineage.

## History · Trust · Auditability

- Prior Experiences remain byte-for-byte unchanged  
- Reflective summaries are template/evaluative, not chain-of-thought dumps  
- Validation harness records reflection creation and outcome codes  

## Future Learning

Learning will read Reflective Experiences as structured observations (contradictions, questions, hypotheses, confidence assessments) — without Reflection mutating the past.

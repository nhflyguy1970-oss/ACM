## Purpose

Explain how Associations prepare ACM for future analogical cognition.  
**Do not** implement analogy engines in M4.

## Similarity & difference

- **Similarity** emerges from shared parents (`is_a`), co-activation, `resembles` links, and overlapping neighborhood membership.  
- **Difference** remains available via conflicting relations (`conflicts_with`), distinct attributes on Concepts, and missing shared neighbors.

## Analogy & transfer (future)

Cross-domain leaps (fly tying → craftsmanship → woodworking → tool use) require:

1. Distant but real Associations (far / unexpected bands)  
2. Structure that can align relation patterns across neighborhoods  
3. Directional strengths that don’t force false symmetry  

M4’s living Associations, distances, and neighborhoods are the cognitive bridges those alignments will walk.

## Pattern correspondence

Future organs may match:

```
A —rel→ B          A' —rel→ B'
```

across domains. Association kinds are therefore evolvable labels, not a frozen mega-ontology.

## Boundary

M4 ships foundations only. Creativity, analogy completion, and transfer learning remain later milestones.
